4. Cultural Story
Coffee is one of El Salvador's most important export products, making it essential to represent the country accurately and respectfully through our design. Our packaging acts as a canvas for Salvadoran pride, featuring national symbols such as the iconic Torogoz bird and native plants, including the Flor de Izote, Maquilishuat, and the coffee plant. The dominant blue tones gracefully reference the Salvadoran flag to communicate freshness, trust, and authenticity to every customer.
