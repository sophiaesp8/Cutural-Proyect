This packaging collection represents El Salvador’s cultural identity through its unique flora, fauna, and coffee-growing heritage from the Apaneca Highlands. 

Each design features national symbols such as the Torogoz bird and native plants, including the Flor de Izote, Maquilishuat, and the coffee plant. Coffee is one of the country’s most important export products, making it essential to represent El Salvador accurately and respectfully through the packaging design. 

The dominant blue tones reference the Salvadoran flag and communicate freshness, trust, and authenticity, while the natural illustrations reinforce the product’s connection to nature and artisanal quality.
